# Intelligence Dashboard

The Intelligence dashboard is your financial command center. It provides a comprehensive overview of your financial position and performance with balance sheet and profit & loss reporting.

## Overview

The Intelligence page shows:

- **Key metrics**: Income, expenses, net income, net worth
- **Balance sheet**: Assets and liabilities as of a specific date
- **Profit & loss**: Income and expenses by category for a date range
- **Key insights**: AI-generated financial insights
- **Date range selection**: View different time periods

## Features

### Financial Metrics

Four key metrics at a glance:

- **Income**: Total income for the selected period
- **Expenses**: Total expenses for the selected period
- **Net Income**: Income minus expenses (positive = profit, negative = loss)
- **Net Worth**: Total assets minus total liabilities

### Balance Sheet (Position)

Shows your financial position as of a specific date:

- **Assets**: All asset accounts with current balances
  - Checking accounts, savings, investments, etc.
  - Total assets displayed
- **Liabilities**: All liability accounts with current balances
  - Credit cards, loans, mortgages, etc.
  - Total liabilities displayed
- **Net Worth**: Assets minus liabilities

### Profit & Loss (Performance)

Shows income and expenses for a date range:

- **Income by category**: Hierarchical tag breakdown of all income
- **Expenses by category**: Hierarchical tag breakdown of all expenses
- **Category totals**: See spending by tag with percentages
- **Visual indicators**: Color-coded progress bars showing category proportions
- **Clickable categories**: Drill down to see transactions for any category

### Date Range Selection

Choose from preset ranges or custom dates:

- **This Month**: Current month to date
- **Last Month**: Previous full month
- **Last 3 Months**: Rolling 3-month period
- **This Year**: Year to date
- **Last Year**: Previous full year
- **Custom**: Select any start and end date

### Key Insights

AI-generated insights appear at the top:

- Spending alerts and trends
- Recommendations for improvement
- Unusual activity detection
- Monthly/quarterly report summaries
- Click through to view all insights

### Category Drill-Down

Click any category to see transactions:

- Filtered by the selected category
- Limited to the date range shown
- Quick navigation to transaction details

## Use Cases

- Monitor overall financial health
- Track spending by category
- Compare periods (this month vs last month)
- Identify major spending categories
- Review income sources
- Calculate net worth
- Generate financial reports

## Understanding the Data

### What's Included

- **Balance Sheet**: All asset and liability accounts
- **P&L**: Income and expense accounts only
- **Excluded**: Transfers between accounts, internal movements
- **Tag exclusions**: Tags marked as "exclude from P&L" are filtered out

### Category Hierarchy

- Parent categories show totals including all children
- Expandable tree structure for detailed breakdown
- Percentages show proportion of total income/expenses

## Related Features

- **Accounts**: Balance sheet data comes from account balances
- **Transactions**: P&L data comes from transaction entries
- **Tags**: Categories are organized by tag hierarchy
- **Insights**: AI insights complement the dashboard
- **Reports**: Monthly/quarterly reports provide deeper analysis
