# Subscriptions & Free Trial

Probably offers flexible subscription options with a generous free trial period to help you experience the full value of the platform.

## Free Trial

### 45-Day Free Trial

New users get a **45-day free trial** to explore all of Probably's features without any commitment.

**Why 45 Days?**
We believe you need a full month to truly understand how game-changing Probably is. The extended trial period gives you time to:
- Connect your bank accounts and import transactions
- Set up your accounts and categories
- Experience automatic categorization and AI insights
- See your financial intelligence reports
- Try the AI chat assistant
- Understand the full value before committing

**Trial Eligibility:**
- One trial per user account
- Trial automatically converts to a paid subscription after 45 days
- No credit card required during trial period
- Full access to all features during trial

## Subscription Plans

### Active Subscription Required

After your trial period ends, an active subscription is required to continue using Probably. This ensures we can continue building and improving the platform.

**Subscription Features:**
- All features remain fully accessible
- Automatic renewal (unless canceled)
- Cancel anytime from your billing settings
- No interruption to your data or account

## Managing Your Subscription

### Billing Settings

Access your subscription management from **Settings → Billing**:
- View your current subscription status
- See trial expiration date (if applicable)
- Manage subscription and payment methods
- Cancel subscription (access continues until period ends)

### Subscription Status

Your subscription can be in one of these states:
- **Trialing**: Currently in your 45-day free trial
- **Active**: Paid subscription is active and current
- **Canceled**: Subscription canceled but still active until period ends
- **Past Due**: Payment issue - contact support

## Why Subscriptions?

Subscriptions enable us to:
- Continuously improve and add new features
- Maintain secure bank connections and infrastructure
- Provide reliable AI-powered financial intelligence
- Support customer success and respond to feedback
- Keep your financial data secure and private

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** for a new account

3. **Connect your accounts** and explore features
4. **Experience the value** over a full month
5. **Subscribe** when ready (or trial converts automatically)
