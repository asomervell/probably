# My Life

The My Life settings page lets you define the important people, work relationships, and assets in your life. This information helps Probably's AI better understand and categorize your transactions.

## How It Works

By telling Probably about your life context, the AI can:

1. **Identify transfers vs. payments** - Sending money to your partner is a transfer, not an expense
2. **Categorize salary correctly** - Income from your employer is recognized as salary
3. **Track related expenses** - Pet expenses, vehicle costs, and property maintenance can be properly grouped
4. **Improve accuracy** - Understanding your context leads to better AI categorization

## Relationship Categories

### People

Track important people in your life:

- **Partner/Spouse** - For joint finances and transfers
- **Parent** - Family money movements
- **Child** - Allowances, school expenses
- **Sibling** - Family transfers
- **Friend** - Personal loans, shared expenses
- **Other** - Any other important person

**Use cases:**
- Money sent to your partner appears as a transfer, not an expense
- Payments from family members categorized correctly
- Shared expenses properly tracked

### Work

Define your professional relationships:

- **Employer** - Your primary income source
- **Client** - Freelance or consulting income
- **Contractor** - People you pay for work
- **Business Partner** - Shared business finances
- **Other** - Any work-related entity

**Use cases:**
- Salary deposits correctly identified
- Client payments recognized as income
- Contractor payments categorized as business expenses

### Assets & Things

Track important assets:

- **Vehicle** - Cars, motorcycles, boats
- **Property** - Houses, apartments, land
- **Pet** - Dogs, cats, other pets
- **Equipment** - Computers, tools, appliances
- **Other** - Any other significant asset

**Use cases:**
- Fuel purchases linked to your vehicle
- Pet store visits categorized as pet expenses
- Property maintenance tracked separately

## Managing Relationships

### Adding Relationships

1. Navigate to **Settings → My Life**
2. Choose the category (People, Work, or Assets)
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Sarah", "Acme Corp", "My Tesla")
4. Select the relationship type from the dropdown
5. Click **Add**

### Removing Relationships

1. Find the relationship in the list
2. Click **Remove** next to it
3. The relationship is deleted immediately

## Best Practices

- **Start with key people** - Add your partner, employer, and anyone you regularly send money to
- **Use clear names** - "Sarah (Wife)" or "John Smith" are better than "S"
- **Add your employer** - Helps identify salary vs. other income
- **Track expensive assets** - Vehicles and pets often have recurring expenses

## Privacy Note

Your relationship data is stored securely and used only to improve your personal categorization. It's never shared or used for any other purpose.
