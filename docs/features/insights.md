# AI Financial Insights

Insights are AI-generated observations about your financial data. They help you understand trends, spot anomalies, and get recommendations for improving your financial health.

## Overview

Insights are automatically generated by analyzing your transactions, spending patterns, and financial trends. They appear in:

- **Intelligence dashboard**: Key insights shown prominently
- **Insights page**: Full list of all insights with filtering
- **Reports**: Insights included in monthly/quarterly reports

## Insight Types

### Spending Alerts

Warnings about unusual or high spending:

- "Your spending on Restaurants increased 40% this month"
- "You've spent $500 on Coffee this month, above your average"
- Alerts when spending exceeds thresholds or historical patterns

### Trends

Observations about spending patterns over time:

- "Your Transportation costs have been decreasing over the past 3 months"
- "Income from Freelance work has increased 25% this quarter"
- Identifies upward or downward trends in categories

### Recommendations

Actionable suggestions for financial improvement:

- "Consider setting a budget for Entertainment spending"
- "You could save $200/month by reducing Restaurant expenses"
- "Your Emergency Fund is below recommended 3 months of expenses"

### Anomalies

Detection of unusual activity:

- "Unusual transaction: $500 at Electronics Store (not typical)"
- "Large income deposit detected"
- Flags transactions or patterns that stand out

### Summaries

High-level overviews of financial periods:

- Monthly report summaries
- Quarterly financial summaries
- Annual overviews

## Features

### Insight Management

- **View all insights**: Complete list with filtering options
- **Filter by type**: Spending alerts, trends, recommendations, etc.
- **Filter by importance**: Show only high-importance insights
- **Dismiss insights**: Remove insights you've addressed
- **View dismissed**: Option to see previously dismissed insights

### Insight Details

Each insight includes:

- **Content**: The insight text explaining the observation
- **Type**: Category of insight (alert, trend, recommendation, etc.)
- **Importance**: Score from 1-10 indicating significance
- **Key badge**: Marked insights are most important
- **Date**: When the insight was generated

### Reports

Monthly and quarterly reports include:

- **Summary**: High-level overview of the period
- **Highlights**: Key positive and negative points
- **Recommendations**: Actionable suggestions
- **Comparison**: Notes comparing to previous period
- **Category breakdown**: Spending by category
- **Report insights**: Insights specific to that period

## Use Cases

- Get notified about unusual spending
- Understand spending trends over time
- Receive personalized financial recommendations
- Identify areas for cost reduction
- Track progress toward financial goals
- Review monthly/quarterly financial summaries

## How Insights Are Generated

Insights are created by:

1. **Transaction analysis**: Reviewing all transactions in a period
2. **Pattern detection**: Identifying trends and anomalies
3. **AI processing**: Using LLM (xAI Grok) to generate natural language insights
4. **Context awareness**: Considering your historical data and patterns
5. **Importance scoring**: Ranking insights by significance

## Best Practices

- Review insights regularly (weekly or monthly)
- Act on recommendations that make sense for your situation
- Dismiss insights you've addressed or that aren't relevant
- Use insights to identify areas for budget adjustments
- Track progress on recommendations over time

## Pulse Dashboard

The Pulse page provides quick financial health indicators:

### Left to Spend

Shows remaining budget for the current period based on income minus expenses and upcoming bills.

### Upcoming Bills

Lists expected recurring payments coming up, based on detected patterns.

### Spending Pace

A line chart comparing your spending trajectory this month versus last month:

- **Visual comparison**: Current month (indigo) vs last month (gray) cumulative spending
- **Pace status**: Shows if you're spending faster, slower, or on track
- **Percentage change**: How much faster or slower compared to the same point last month
- **Largest expenses**: Top 5 transactions contributing to this month's spending

The spending pace excludes transfers between accounts and only includes actual expenses.
