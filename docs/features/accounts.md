# Accounts

Accounts are the foundation of your financial tracking in Probably. They represent the different places where your money lives and flows.

## Account Types

Probably uses double-entry bookkeeping with four main account types:

- **Assets**: Money you own (checking accounts, savings accounts, cash, investments)
- **Liabilities**: Money you owe (credit cards, loans, mortgages)
- **Income**: Money coming in (salary, freelance income, investment returns)
- **Expenses**: Money going out (groceries, rent, utilities)

## Features

### Manual Account Creation

Create accounts manually to track any financial account:

- Name your account (e.g., "Chase Checking", "Amex Credit Card")
- Select the account type
- Optionally set an opening balance
- Add notes or descriptions

### Bank Account Integration

Connect real bank accounts via multiple providers:

- **Multi-provider support**: Connect through Teller, Plaid, or Akahu
- **Secure, read-only access**: Bank-level encryption and security
- **Automatic account creation**: Accounts created when connecting
- **Institution information**: Logos and names automatically populated
- **Real-time balance syncing**: Balances updated automatically
- **Provider-agnostic**: Same experience regardless of provider

See [Multi-Provider Bank Connections](bank-connections.md) for details on connecting accounts.

### Account Management

- View all accounts organized by type
- See current balances for each account
- View transaction history for any account
- Edit account details (name, type, notes)
- Delete accounts (with safety checks)

### Balance Tracking

Accounts automatically track their balance based on transactions:

- Balances update as you add transactions
- View balance as of any date (for historical reporting)
- Separate tracking for assets vs liabilities
- Net worth calculation (assets - liabilities)

## Use Cases

- Track multiple checking and savings accounts
- Monitor credit card balances
- Manage investment accounts
- Track loans and mortgages
- Organize income sources
- Categorize expense accounts

## Related Features

- **Transactions**: All transactions are linked to accounts
- **Intelligence**: Account balances feed into balance sheet reporting
- **Bank Connections**: Automatically creates accounts from connected banks (Teller, Plaid, Akahu)
- **Transfers**: Track money movement between accounts
