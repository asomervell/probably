### Prefer Apple-Touch-Icon Over og:image for Logo Extraction

Improved logo extraction by prioritizing apple-touch-icon meta tags, which typically provide higher quality icons than Open Graph images.

**What's New:**
- Updated Firecrawl client to check for apple-touch-icon before og:image
- Enhanced logo extraction logic to prefer higher quality sources
- Improved fallback chain for logo discovery

**Why This Matters:**
Apple touch icons are typically designed specifically for app icons and are usually higher resolution and better quality than Open Graph images. This improvement results in better logo quality for merchants and entities throughout the application.

**Technical Details:**
- Enhanced `internal/enrichment/firecrawl_client.go` logo extraction logic
- Updated meta tag parsing to prioritize apple-touch-icon
- Improved fallback sequence for logo discovery
