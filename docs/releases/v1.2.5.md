## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### Resolve Merge Conflicts and Exclude Past Bills from Pulse Calculations

Fixed merge conflicts and improved Pulse dashboard accuracy by excluding past bills and investment accounts from calculations.

**What's New:**
- Resolved merge conflicts in worker.go
- Exclude bills with dates in the past from upcoming bills list
- Exclude accounts with 'investment' in name from left to spend calculation
- Increased upcoming bills window from 30 to 60 days to show more bills
- Added comprehensive test coverage for various components

**Why This Matters:**
The Pulse dashboard should only show upcoming bills, not bills that have already passed. Investment accounts shouldn't be included in "left to spend" calculations since they're not part of day-to-day spending. These fixes make the Pulse dashboard more accurate and useful for financial planning.

**Technical Details:**
- Resolved merge conflicts in `internal/processing/worker.go`
- Updated `internal/pulse/calculator.go` to filter past bills and investment accounts
- Enhanced `internal/insights/aggregator.go` with improved date filtering
- Added extensive test coverage in `internal/handlers/api_extended_test.go`, `internal/handlers/api_workflows_test.go`, `internal/insights/aggregator_test.go`, and other test files
- Updated `internal/handlers/insights.go` and `internal/handlers/transactions.go` with improved logic
