Your financial data is yours. Period. You shouldn't have to wonder what happens if a service shuts down, or if you want to move to something else, or if you just want a copy of your own records.

**Full backup and restore is now live.**

One click exports everything: accounts, transactions, tags, rules, merchants, recurring patterns. It's a ZIP file with clean JSON inside—human-readable, machine-parseable, completely portable. You can open it in any text editor and see exactly what's there.

Restore is just as simple. Upload a backup file, and everything comes back exactly as it was. Migrating between devices, setting up a new instance, recovering from disaster—all covered.

This isn't just a feature. It's a promise: your financial history will never be trapped.

**Data portability as a first-class citizen.**
