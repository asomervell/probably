Most finance apps make you work for answers. Want to know how much you spent on groceries last month? Click through categories, filter by date, maybe export to a spreadsheet. Want to compare this quarter to last? More clicking, more filtering, more mental math.

**Now you can just ask.**

Probably's AI Chat understands natural language questions about your finances. Ask "How much did I spend on coffee last month?" and get an instant answer with a clear breakdown. Ask "What's my biggest expense category this year?" and see it visualized immediately.

The AI generates the right queries, executes them securely on your data, and presents results in plain English with charts and tables. No SQL knowledge required. No clicking through menus. Just ask what you want to know.

Questions like "Show me all transactions over $500" or "How does my spending this month compare to last month?" work instantly. The system understands context, handles complex queries, and gives you answers formatted the way you'd want to see them.

**Your financial data, finally conversational.**
