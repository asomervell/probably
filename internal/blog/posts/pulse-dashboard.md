Most financial dashboards show you what already happened. Your balance, last month's spending, historical trends. That's useful, but it doesn't answer the question you actually have: "Can I afford this?" or "What's coming up that I need to plan for?"

**Pulse is different. It's forward-looking.**

Pulse shows you what's coming up: bills due in the next 60 days, recurring subscriptions, upcoming expenses. It calculates how much you have left to spend this month based on your income, upcoming bills, and current balance. It's the dashboard that helps you make decisions, not just review the past.

At a glance, you can see: your available spending money, what bills are coming due, and how much buffer you have. No mental math. No clicking through accounts. Just a clear answer to "Am I okay this month?" and "What do I need to plan for?"

This is now the hero dashboard when you log in. Open Probably, see your financial pulse, make informed decisions.

**Financial planning, not just financial history.**
