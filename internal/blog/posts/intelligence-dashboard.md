The first question everyone has when they open a finance app is simple: "Am I okay?" Most apps make you work for the answer—click through accounts, add up balances, remember what you owe, do mental math.

**The Intelligence Dashboard answers that question instantly.**

One screen shows your net worth (assets minus liabilities), your month-to-date spending versus last month, and any anomalies the AI has spotted. No clicking through. No mental math. Just the answer.

Below that, you'll see AI-generated insights: spending that's trending up, subscriptions that quietly increased, categories where you're over or under your usual patterns. The system watches so you don't have to.

This is now the default landing page after you log in. Open Probably, see your position, get on with your day.

**Financial clarity in under 5 seconds.**
