Bank transaction descriptions are optimized for processing, not reading. "TST* COFFEE SHOP 4521" could be anywhere. "AMZN MKTP US*2X4Y7Z" is technically Amazon. "SQ *RANDOM WORDS" is Square—but for which business?

Your transaction list should be as readable as a receipt from your favorite store.

**We now automatically enrich every transaction with clean merchant names and logos.**

That cryptic "TST* AUSTIN ROASTERS 4452" becomes "Austin Roasters" with their actual logo. Amazon purchases show the Amazon logo. Your local coffee shop shows up by name, not by payment processor code.

It sounds simple, but it transforms the experience. You can actually scan your transactions and know what happened. No more squinting at abbreviations or Googling merchant codes.

**Your money, finally readable.**
