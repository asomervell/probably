You already use ChatGPT for questions about code, writing, research, and planning. What if you could ask it about your finances too?

**Probably now integrates with ChatGPT Apps.**

Connect your Probably account to ChatGPT, and suddenly you can ask natural language questions about your money right in ChatGPT's interface. "How much did I spend on restaurants last month?" "What's my net worth?" "Show me my recurring subscriptions." All answered instantly with rich, interactive visualizations.

The integration uses OAuth 2.1 with PKCE for security—your financial data stays private and secure. ChatGPT can only access what you authorize, and you can revoke access anytime. It's the same secure architecture that powers Probably's web interface, just accessible through ChatGPT's conversational interface.

When ChatGPT answers questions about your finances, it doesn't just give you text—it shows interactive widgets: spending summaries with category breakdowns, account balance cards, financial overview dashboards, and transaction search results. Your financial data, visualized beautifully, right in the conversation.

This isn't just convenience—it's a fundamentally different way to interact with your finances. Instead of opening an app, navigating menus, and clicking through reports, you just ask. The same conversational interface you use for everything else now understands your financial life.

**Your finances, wherever you already are.**
